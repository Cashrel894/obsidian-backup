#calculus
$\int x^\alpha\, dx =\frac{x^{\alpha+1}}{\alpha+1}$

$\int \frac{1}{x} dx = \ln |x| + C$

$\int e^{ x }dx = e^{ x } + C$

$\int a^{x}dx=\frac{a^{x}}{\ln a}+C$

$\int \cos xdx=\sin x+C$

$\int \sin xdx=-\cos x+C$

$\int \frac{1}{\cos ^{2}x}dx=\tan x+C$

$\int \frac{1}{\sin ^{2}x}dx=-\cot x+C$

$\int \frac{\sin x}{\cos ^{2}x}dx=\int \tan x\sec xdx=\sec x+C$

$\int \frac{\cos x}{\sin ^{2}x}dx=\int \cot x\csc xdx=-\csc x+C$

$\int \frac{1}{\sqrt{ a^{2}-x^{2} }}dx=\arcsin \frac{x}{a}+C$

$\int \frac{1}{a^{2}+x^{2}}dx=\frac{1}{a}\arctan \frac{x}{a}+C$

$\int \frac{1}{x^{2}-a^{2}}dx=\frac{1}{2a}\ln| \frac{x-a}{x+a} |+C$

$\int \frac{1}{x^{2}\pm a^{2}}dx=\ln| x+\sqrt{ x^{2}\pm a^{2} } |+C$

$\int \frac{1}{\sqrt{ x^{2}\pm a^{2} }}dx=\ln|x+\sqrt{ x^{2}\pm a^{2} }|+C$
 
$\int \sqrt{ a^{2}-x^{2} }dx=\frac{x}{2}\sqrt{ a^{2}-x^{2} }+\frac{a^{2}}{2}\arcsin \frac{x}{a} +C$

$\int \sqrt{ x^{2}\pm a^{2} }dx=\frac{x}{2}\sqrt{ x^{2}\pm a^{2}}\pm\frac{a^{2}}{2}\ln| x+\sqrt{ x^{2}\pm a^{2} } |+C$

$\int \sec xdx=\ln|\sec x+\tan x|+C$

$\int \csc xdx=\ln|\csc x-\cot x|+C$

$\int \sinh xdx=\cosh x+C$

$\int \cosh xdx=\sinh x+C$
